About President Museveni

The People's President

Yoweri Kaguta Museveni has been the President of Uganda since 1986, leading the country through significant transformation and development. Born in 1944 in Ntungamo, he played a crucial role in the liberation struggle that ousted dictatorships and brought peace to Uganda.

Under his leadership, Uganda has seen remarkable progress in infrastructure, education, healthcare, and economic growth. His vision for the 2026-2031 term focuses on job creation, industrialization, and continued peace and stability.

Parish Development Model (PDM)

Transforming Uganda from the Parish Level

The Parish Development Model (PDM) is President Museveni's flagship program to eradicate poverty by delivering services and funding directly to Uganda's 10,594 parishes.

PDM focuses on 7 pillars: Economic Infrastructure, Financial Inclusion, Social Services, Community Mobilization, Governance & Administration, Production & Marketing, and Data Management.

UGX 100M per Parish
